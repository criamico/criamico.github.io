A bit of history

I got a B.Sc. in Electronic Engineering and a M.Sc. in Automation Engineering in my hometown of Catania, Italy. After college I decided that I wanted to do software development.

In 2010 I moved to Turin where I got my first job as electronic systems engineer, then after a brief period I started working as software developer in the automotive industry.

In 2013 I moved to the Greater Detroit Area, USA, to work as consultant for FCA. I was in charge of the UI development for instruments cluster software. You can see some of the projects I worked on here (Dodge Challenger 2015), here (Chrysler 300) and here (Maserati Quattroporte 2014). I enjoyed a lot working with UI and I understood that is the role for me, since it needs technical skills as well as creativity.

In 2015 I moved again, this time to Dublin, where I started using my UI experience to create front-end projects. I decided to transition to state-of-the-art web technologies (Html5, Css3, Javascript), modern paradigms (responsive design) and widely used frameworks (AngularJS).
